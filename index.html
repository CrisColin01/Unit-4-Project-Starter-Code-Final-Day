Things about Vegetta777

Samuel de Luque Batuecas (born 12 April 1989), known online as Vegetta777, is a Spanish gaming YouTuber known for his Minecraft videos. His YouTube channel is the 2nd most-subscribed channel from Spain.

About his life

Vegetta777 or Samuel de Luque lives alone in his apartment with his cat Kira and gets up in the morning to do exercise and later does a LIVE on his YouTube channel, Samuel throughout his YouTuber career has never failed a day without uploading a video or do a LIVE and that for me is something admirable since he takes his work seriously.
